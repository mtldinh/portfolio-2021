NCAAT and PIVOT Internship

As a youth intern, I brainstormed voter engagement content, hosted and created community events, and networked with Asian Americans nationwide for the 2020 US national election. I worked with North Carolina Asian Americans Together (NCAAT), their political C4 NCAAT in Action, and the Progressive Vietnamese American Organization (PIVOT). Under PIVOT, I was a youth Vietnamese American fellow representing the swing state, North Carolina.

During my virtual time as a voter engagement intern:

  • I co-created a youth voter engagement event, “Ghoul Down with NCAAT” to encourage youth to textbank to others in the North Carolina community.
  • I researched and designed a Carrd website on slacktivism, in an effort to inform others on the complexities of social media activism. For this, I created a survey that asked participants on their opinions of social media activism.
  • I contributed to creating voter engagement mailers that were sent to over 1000 Vietnamese Americans in North Carolina
  • I co-hosted several virtual events for Vietnamese Americans that phonebanked or textbanked over 8000 Vietnamese Americans in North Carolina
  • I wrote for Vietnamese information campaigns surrounding misinformation in the 2020 election, specifically for VietAmVote and VietFactCheck (add photos)

This was my first time working with non-profit organizations, and it won’t be my last. I am incredibly grateful for the opportunity to engage in Asian American advocacy and community, and look forward to future endeavors!
