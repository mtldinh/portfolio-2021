Eaton Power Systems Internship

Eaton Corporation is a power management company whose mission is to improve the efficiencies, safeties, and sustainabilities of numerous products and services.

I worked virtually for the Warrendale, Pennsylvania facility as a power systems engineering intern in the summer of 2021. For in-person opportunities, I was based in Raleigh, North Carolina.

As a power systems engineering intern, I performed the following:

  • Assisted in eight short circuit studies, coordination studies, and arc flash analysis with SKM power software
  • Created seven quotes of engineering services for customers
  • Automated a document template using Visual Basic Applications (VBA) for Microsoft Word
  • Attended a pre-bid walkthrough for electrical systems in a customer's facility

Despite the virtual setting, working at Eaton was an incredibly rewarding experience. It strengthened my desire to continue studying electrical engineering, especially as I was hesitant while taking online classes due to COVID-19. I also deeply appreciate Eaton's continued efforts for a safe and comfortable environment. I got to meet many kind and amazing people throughout my time there.

I will be returning in the summer of 2022 as a field service engineering intern. If you would like more information about my work with Eaton, please contact me!
